GET INVOLVED

HOW TO GET ON THE MIC

Want to get roasted? Here's how:

01

WATCH OUR STORY

Follow @bananaphonemic on Instagram. We post sign-up prompts on Fridays (and sometimes Saturdays).

02

DROP YOUR NAME

When you see the "story questions" prompt, drop your name in the box. That's it. Don't DM us — we don't read them for sign-ups.

03

CHECK THE LIST

If you made the show, you'll be tagged in an Instagram story by 6PM on Sunday (day of the show).

04

SHOW UP & CHECK IN

Head to The Creek & The Cave backyard. Check-in at 9:30 PM. You MUST check your name by 9:40 PM or you'll be replaced!

Didn't make the list? Show up anyway! The stand-by list is on the same sheet. Spots open up!
